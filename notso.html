A Not so in love: Love story. A heart warming and crumbling story about love,regret,second chances and last minute changes of loved ones

Isleen Mortain wasn't looking for a second chance.

She was planning a wedding.

Engaged to a man who loves her wholeheartedly, she had long ago accepted that some stories are meant to remain unfinished. The past belonged where it was, and the future was already waiting for her.

Until a familiar face walked back into her life.

Suddenly, forgotten conversations, lingering glances, and years of unanswered questions begin to surface. What starts as nostalgia slowly becomes something far more dangerous: the realization that certain feelings never truly disappear.

Now, standing between the life she's promised and the one she never got to explore, she must decide whether love is about holding on, letting go, or finding the courage to live with the consequences of either choice.

A story about timing, choice, and the people who leave fingerprints on our hearts long after they're gone.
