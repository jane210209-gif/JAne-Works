Delaney Marinos has built her career on facts, evidence, and winning impossible cases. As one of the most respected young lawyers in the country, she has little time for distractions.

Except for one.

A small podcast with barely any listeners.

Hosted anonymously by a man whose voice has become a familiar companion during late nights at the office, the show is thoughtful, honest, and strangely comforting. Delaney never misses an episode, even if no one else seems to know it exists.

Then she takes on a high-profile case for the second son of the powerful Sterling family.

The last thing she expects is to discover that the family's elusive third heir is the voice she's been listening to for years.

Lyle Sterling is nothing like the headlines that follow his name. Quiet where others are loud, observant where others seek attention, he keeps his podcast hidden from the world for reasons of his own. But when Delaney unknowingly becomes part of his life, the distance between anonymous conversations and reality begins to disappear.

As a complicated legal battle unfolds and family pressures mount, Delaney and Lyle find themselves drawn together by the one place they had already known each other all along: a voice in the dark and someone who was listening.

A story about unexpected connections, hidden truths, and finding the person you've been searching for without ever knowing their name.
